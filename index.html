About me

I've embarked on an exhilarating journey, transitioning from a three-decade corporate career to pursue my true passion: inspiring middle and high school girls to delve into the world of STEM. With a wealth of experience under my belt, I've redirected my focus towards fostering enthusiasm and confidence in these young minds, advocating for greater diversity and representation in STEM fields. My mission is to ignite a spark of curiosity and empowerment, guiding the next generation of female leaders towards exciting opportunities in science, technology, engineering, and mathematics..

  • Community

    Backyard Produce Project

    As an active member of my community, I am deeply invested in various initiatives aimed at fostering inclusivity and support for those in need. Guided by a genuine passion for social justice, I take pride in my role in facilitating the integration of new refugees into our society. By providing practical assistance, emotional support, and access to resources, I strive to make their transition smoother and more manageable. Additionally, I am committed to addressing the pressing issue of food insecurity among low-income families. Recognizing the profound impact of hunger on individuals and communities, I am actively involved in an initiative that provide fresh produce to those facing financial hardship. In both these endeavors, my goal is to create a more compassionate and inclusive community where everyone has the opportunity to thrive. Through hands-on involvement and collaborative efforts, I seek to make a meaningful difference in the lives of those facing adversity, fostering a sense of belonging and solidarity within our community.
